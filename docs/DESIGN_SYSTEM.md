# MK Design System Foundation

Phase 3 establishes a restrained MK visual direction rather than a final brand polish.

## Visual direction

- Serious, advisory and board-ready.
- Near-black and warm cream as the base.
- Brass accents used sparingly for trust, priority and action.
- Spacious layouts with calm hierarchy.
- No neon, gimmicks or SaaS clutter.

## Tokens

Defined in `tailwind.config.ts` under `colors.mk`:

- `ink`
- `charcoal`
- `slate`
- `brass`
- `brassDark`
- `cream`
- `paper`
- `line`
- `muted`
- `danger`
- `success`
